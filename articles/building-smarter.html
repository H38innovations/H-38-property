Building smarter: a new lens on modular design in Australia

Conversation By Priya Cardwell • March 21, 2025

Kit-of-parts for rapid delivery

Modular delivery unlocks consistent quality for regional communities. Fabricated in controlled environments, components arrive ready to assemble, speeding delivery while keeping waste in check.

Community-first programming

Modular spaces can flex as communities grow. By designing for adaptive reuse from the outset, the structures evolve over decades without heavy material footprints.
